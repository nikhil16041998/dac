Q22) Explain Friend function?
ans:-friend Function: Friend are functions or whole classes which are granted access to the private data of a class.This breaks the encapsulation.

It is a non-member function.
Has access to all the Private and Protected members of a class.
Friend class: One class friend of another class.

syntax: friend class class_name;

The friend class and all of its member functions have access to the private members defined within the other class.
provides additional functionality from outside the class.
